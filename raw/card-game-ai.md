Three ai behavior you can select depending on the deck:

- Aggro: Tries more to attack face a lot, tries more to evoke the most possible number of demons to overcome the enemy
- Control: Tries more to control the board with removal spells and effect, priority is to clear the enemy board so can overwhelm him. Prioritize removing enemy biggest threat.
- Ramp: Tries always to put in play big demons so will conserve and play card that make mana to play big demons.
- Midrange: Balanced approach
