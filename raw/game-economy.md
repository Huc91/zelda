Every shop sells:
packs, singles.
Basic card shop selling value:
common: 200
rare: 800
epic: 1500
legendary: 5000
foil: that value x 100.
This is for buying.
For singles every day the single he has on stock changes. He sells just 6 single a day
2 commons, 2 rare, 1 epic, 1 mythic. he take that price and apply a variance from -25% to +25%. He sells just one copy a day.
Next day the cards and prices changes.

Wanted cards, every day each shop looks for 6 different cards.
Basic card, selling to shop value:
common: 2
rare: 8
epic: 15
legendary: 50
foil: that value x 100.

But if the the card is one of the wanted from the shop, shop pays this value x 100, with a a variance from -25% to +25%. He buys just one copy a day.

In the same shop, selled cards and wanted cards can never coincide. But a card can be wanted in shop 1 and selled in shop 2, this incentivize economy.
