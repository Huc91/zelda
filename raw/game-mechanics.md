The game is a bit like Zelda but with card battles.
The map is an archipealgo with 5 islands.
You need to to defeat the boss of each island to win the game and then the final boss.
You can find powerups around to traverse part you could not traverse before.
Combat is card game, but there is a part real time when who attacks first the enemy gets an advntage in battle in the initiative for the battle.
You open packs to find cards, but is also exploration heavy. some cards only found in the wild in hidden places, etc...
