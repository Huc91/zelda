# Meta

## Base set only

### Death Ping

deathping.png

#### strategy

Put death knell in rear and protect it at all costs
spam enemy with cheap attacker, destroy their demons and kill him with ping effect from death knell. Death Knell is the cornerstone of the strategy.

### Tolarian Terror

terrospell.png

#### strategy

Fill the GY with cheap spell to play Tidal Terror for free.
play pyromancer to play cheap spells and wrath enemy board.

### Reanimator

renimator.png

#### strategy

For this deck makes sense to discard hand (keep cards at the end of turn and not using the arsenal to put cards in the GY) Prioritize putting strong demons in the GY then use reanimating spells like resurrection or final hour. Final hour is the perfect arsenal card.
For chaos king dragon put it in arsenal if you do not have enough obscura and regalia cards to summon him. You need to have at least 3 obscura and 3 regalia in GY to play chaos king dragon.
