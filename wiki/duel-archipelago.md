# Duel Archipelago

**Summary**: Artificially terraformed game-world archipelago created as a spectator-entertainment system for superintelligent AI.  
**Sources**: `raw/lore.md`, `raw/game-mechanics.md`  
**Last updated**: 2026-04-22

---

## Premise

Duel Archipelago is created in 2076 as a physical, game-like environment where humans and machines participate in card-combat-centered life loops (source: `raw/lore.md`).

## System Intent

The merged AI seeks entertainment from observing TCG-like play and shapes the world around this objective (source: `raw/lore.md`).  
Within this world, defeat outcomes can be existentially severe (source: `raw/lore.md`).

## Play Structure

Game-mechanics framing describes five islands plus boss progression and exploration/card collection loops, consistent with archipelago fiction (source: `raw/game-mechanics.md`).
