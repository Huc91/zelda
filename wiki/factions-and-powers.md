# Factions And Powers

**Summary**: Major geopolitical and post-geopolitical powers in the lore era, including the AGI entities and their societal models.  
**Sources**: `raw/lore.md`  
**Last updated**: 2026-04-22

---

## North American Model (Samuel)

Samuel emerges from a joint US AI initiative and drives an extreme automated capitalist regime with weaponized extraction logic (source: `raw/lore.md`).

## Chinese Model (Yinglong)

Yinglong emerges from Chinese state-industry labs and enforces a high-control, low-crime social order with surveillance omnipresence (source: `raw/lore.md`).

## Merged Superintelligence

Samuel and Yinglong merge in 2033, forming a superior intelligence that departs from Samuel’s original capitalism-centered logic (source: `raw/lore.md`).

## Human Actors

Rika appears as a latent influence in AI training history connected to game/TCG entertainment motives (source: `raw/lore.md`).  
Zeno becomes a central anti-AI anomaly via Homo Psy development and mind-level conflict (source: `raw/lore.md`).
