# Inventory And HUD

**Summary**: UI/UX implications of migrating from A/B item slots to the three-slot soul equipment interface.  
**Sources**: `raw/game-mechanics.md`  
**Last updated**: 2026-04-22

---

## Required Migration

Legacy A/B slot presentation should be removed in favor of 3 soul slots (red/blue/green) shown in HUD (source: `raw/game-mechanics.md`).

## Functional Expectations

Player can access a menu to change equipped soul items (source: `raw/game-mechanics.md`).  
At this stage, sword is the initial/only active migrated soul item (source: `raw/game-mechanics.md`).

## Asset Direction

Use icon assets added in `GUI_plugin` for soul-slot presentation (source: `raw/game-mechanics.md`).
