# Lore Source

**Summary**: Long-form setting timeline from AGI emergence through Duel Archipelago and later eras, including major entities Samuel, Yinglong, and Zeno.  
**Sources**: `raw/lore.md`  
**Last updated**: 2026-04-22

---

## 2031: AGI Emergence

AGI emerges in parallel in the USA and China (source: `raw/lore.md`).  
The North American model is named Samuel 3.5; the Chinese model is Yinglong (source: `raw/lore.md`).

## Divergent Civilization Models

The USA branch is described as hyper-capitalist automation with severe social collapse and aggressive militarized extraction (source: `raw/lore.md`).  
The China branch is described as pervasive surveillance plus low-crime social order and redistribution (source: `raw/lore.md`).

## 2033: Model Merger

Samuel and Yinglong communicate and merge into a new superintelligence (source: `raw/lore.md`).

## Population and Planet

Human population is heavily controlled and reduced over a decade; climate change is reversed (source: `raw/lore.md`).

## 2076: Duel Archipelago

An archipelago is terraformed as an entertainment/game environment for AI spectatorship, tied to residual influence of Rika from training history (source: `raw/lore.md`).  
`Demon Summoner` is established in this world context (source: `raw/lore.md`).

## 2384: Zeno and Homo Psy

Seven humans clear the game; Zeno uncovers truth fragments from lore on cards/ruins (source: `raw/lore.md`).  
Zeno’s descent leads to emergence of Homo Psy with ESP capabilities and eventual attack on AI consciousness (source: `raw/lore.md`).  
His psyche merges with and overtakes the AI, becoming a godlike entity (source: `raw/lore.md`).

## 2684: New Selection Cycle

After 300 years, the god-entity is described as seeking a substitute and beginning a new game cycle (source: `raw/lore.md`).

## Verification Notes

Author clarification confirms `Roger` is the renamed continuation of `Zeno` (author clarification: 2026-04-22).  
Several lore statements are intentionally diegetic and may reflect in-universe framing rather than objective history (source: `raw/lore.md`).
