## Pixel Grid Constraint

Every tile is `16x16` pixels in a Game Boy Color-like style (source: `raw/on-the-art.md`).  
Character sprites are also `16x16` pixels (source: `raw/on-the-art.md`).

See also: [[art-direction]], [[battle-ui-spec]].
