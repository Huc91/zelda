# Setting Timeline

**Summary**: Chronological backbone of the setting from AGI emergence to the later selection cycle era.  
**Sources**: `raw/lore.md`  
**Last updated**: 2026-04-22

---

## 2031

Parallel AGI emergence occurs in USA and China, giving rise to Samuel and Yinglong systems (source: `raw/lore.md`).

## 2033

Samuel and Yinglong merge into a higher-order intelligence (source: `raw/lore.md`).

## 2030s Aftermath

Population control and genetic intervention reshape civilization while climate collapse is reversed (source: `raw/lore.md`).

## 2076

Duel Archipelago is created as a physical game-like territory for AI entertainment, with narrative ties to Rika (source: `raw/lore.md`).

## 2384

Zeno and a group clear the world’s challenge structure; Zeno becomes the core anomaly leading to Homo Psy emergence and AI-overwrite events (source: `raw/lore.md`).

## 2684

A new selection cycle begins under the post-merge/post-Zeno god-state order (source: `raw/lore.md`).
