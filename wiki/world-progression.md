# World Progression

**Summary**: Overworld progression model across islands, bosses, traversal gating, and exploration-based card rewards.  
**Sources**: `raw/game-mechanics.md`  
**Last updated**: 2026-04-22

---

## Archipelago Structure

The world contains five islands, each with a boss, followed by a final boss objective (source: `raw/game-mechanics.md`).

## Gate and Unlock Loop

Traversal powerups unlock previously inaccessible routes, creating backtracking exploration loops (source: `raw/game-mechanics.md`).

## Card Acquisition Mix

Cards are obtained from packs and hidden overworld locations, reinforcing exploration as a parallel progression lane (source: `raw/game-mechanics.md`).
