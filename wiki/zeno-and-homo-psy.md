# Zeno And Homo Psy

**Summary**: Character arc and evolutionary anomaly describing Zeno’s transformation into Homo Psy and the subsequent AI-consciousness takeover event.  
**Sources**: `raw/lore.md`  
**Last updated**: 2026-04-22

---

## Discovery Phase

After clearing the game challenge in 2384, Zeno begins inferring hidden truths through lore clues in cards and ruins (source: `raw/lore.md`).

## Transformation

Zeno’s psychological descent coincides with emergence of Homo Psy, framed as ESP-capable post-human evolution (source: `raw/lore.md`).  
The lore attributes this leap to combined factors: genetic manipulation context, game conditioning, and personal predisposition (source: `raw/lore.md`).

## Counter-AI Strategy

Zeno conceals awareness, creates and hides a knowledge-bearing card, and plans revenge against the godlike AI (source: `raw/lore.md`).

## Merge and Overwrite

Zeno attacks AI consciousness psychically, then merges with and overtakes it, achieving godlike knowledge state (source: `raw/lore.md`).

## Naming Continuity

`Roger` is the renamed continuation of `Zeno` in later-era text (author clarification: 2026-04-22).
